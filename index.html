All About Clairo

Keep reading to learn all about the indie world sensation Clairo! We will discuss where she came from, her first hit song, and everything she's done after.

Life Before Fame

Claire Cottrill, also known by her stage name Clairo, was born in Atlanta, Georgia, but only lived there until age 7. Her family briefly moved to Washington for three years, before finally settling into Carlisle, Massachusetts. She was born on August 18th, 1998, and have one sister.

First Song

Her first song to gain attention was "Pretty Girl", a lo-fi single that she recorded a music video to in her bedroom. This gained a lot of traction online, and led to a record deal. After this deal, she released her first EP, Diary 001.

Check out her Pretty Girl Video!

Albums

After the success of her first single and first EP, she released a full length albun, "Immunity," featuring 11 songs with a run time of 41 minutes. She released this album in 2019. Two years later she released her second album, "Sling", with 12 songs and a runtime of 45 minutes. Most recently, she released her third album, "Charm," with 11 songs and a 38 minute play time. She performed songs from all three albums recently at Coachella, a very popular music festival that took place in Indio, California.

Read Javi Zamorano's article on Clairo's third album Charm

Charm seems to be a good in between what she always naturally seemed to be: a fun, melodic and minimalistic indie girl, and what she always meant to become: a powerful, experimental artist and composer. It feels to me like she did this fully for herself, not for the expectations, not for the recognition, but to share exactly how she was feeling at the moment. -Javi Zamorano
